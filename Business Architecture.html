Business Architecture

Baseline Architecture

Baseline Business Goals and Objectives:

  1. Customer Delight:
    Goal:Ensuring exceptional customer satisfaction by consistently delivering top- quality baked goods that exceed customer expectations. Objective: Conduct customer surveys and feedback analysis to understand preferences and improve product offerings to enhance overall satisfaction.

  2. Competitive Pricing:
    Goal:Offering excellent bakery products at competitive and affordable prices to cater to a wide customer base. Objective:Regularly review pricing strategies and market trends to align our pricing models with customer expectations while maintaining product quality and profitability.

  3. Market Expansion:
    Goal:Expanding market reach by considering the establishment of additional outlets, making our bakery more accessible to a larger customer base. Objective:Conduct market research to identify potential locations for new outlets and develop a strategic expansion plan to achieve steady growth over the next few years.

    4. Baseline Business Functions:

    1. Bakery Product Creation:
      Skilled bakers provide a wide range of high-quality products that cater to different tastes and preferences, providing baked products that are consistent and attractive to look at.

    2. Customer Experience and Satisfaction:
      Implementing customer-centric practices and training staff to meet and exceed customer expectations at the bakery fosters satisfaction and loyalty.

    3. Operational Efficiency:
      The bakery prioritizes operational efficiency to deliver high-quality products at affordable prices, streamlining internal processes, managing inventory, and optimizing resource allocation for cost-effectiveness.
