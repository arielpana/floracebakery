Gap Analysis:
Business Process:

Adding Assets and Expanding Product Range

Baseline:

Florace offers a specific range of baked goods and possesses a limited set of equipment and assets for production and service provision.


Target:

The desired outcome is to enhance the bakery's offerings by incorporating a diverse range of bakery products and investing in additional equipment and assets to optimize production and elevate service quality.


Gap:

Failure to upgrade assets and expand the product range could potentially result in decreased customer satisfaction due to limited options, increased operational costs due to inefficiencies, and reduced competitiveness in the bakery industry.
