Organization Description:

A traditional bakery that brings the timeless art of baking to life and we craft delectable, freshly baked goods using time-honored recipes, high-quality ingredients, and a commitment to excellence in taste and presentation.

Mission:

To passionately craft and deliver high-quality baked goods, fostering joy and creating memorable experiences for our customers, every day.

Vision:

To be the most loved and trusted bakery, delighting customer with our exceptional products and exceptional service.

Goals:

  • Quality Excellence:
    Ensure the consistent delivery of top-notch, delicious bakery products that exceed customer expectations.

  • Customer Satisfaction:
    Prioritize customer needs and preferences, aiming for exceptional service and building long-lasting relationships.

  • Sustainable Growth: Expand our reach and product offerings, striving for environmentally sustainable and economically viable business practices.

  • Employee Empowerment: Nurture a positive work environment that encourages growth, creativity, and collaboration among our employees.

Objectives:

To create and deliver freshly baked goods that exceed customer expectations, while maintaining high standards of quality, taste, and presentation.

Organization Chart:


Description of Roles in the Organizational Chart:

  • Owner/Founder:
    Responsible for setting the overall direction of the bakery, making strategic decisions, and overseeing the entire operation.

  • Operation Manager:
    Oversees the day-to-day operations of the bakery, including staff management, inventory control, and ensuring that each branch operates efficiently.

  • Head Pastry Chef:
    Responsible for creating and maintaining the quality and variety of the bakery's cakes and pastries. They lead the pastry teams in each branch and are in charge of recipe development and quality control.

  • Branch Managers (Branch1, 2, 3, and 4):
    Each branch manager is responsible for the day-to-day operations of their respective branch, including sales, customer service, and staff management.

  • Cashier:
    Responsible for handling customer transactions, such as accepting payments, giving change, processing returns, and providing excellent customer service.

  • Chief Baker in Branch:
    Responsible for supervising and managing all aspects of baking and pastry production at the branch and ensures that all baked goods and pastries are prepared according to the bakery's recipes and quality standards.
