Data Architecture

Baseline Data Architecture:

  • Entity-Relationship Diagram (ERD):
    The baseline ERD for Florace Bakery outlines interconnected entities like product, order, customer, employee, transaction, ingredients, and inventory, guiding operations optimization, customer experiences, and efficient inventory and order management processes.




Data Flow Diagram (DFD):


The baseline data flow diagram (DFD) illustrates Florace Bakery's data flow, including order fulfillment, inventory management, product baking, and quality assurance. It connected customers and employees, utilizing product and customer databases for efficient operations and customer satisfaction.



Target Data Architecture:

Entity-Relationship Diagram (ERD):
Florace Bakery has updated its ERD to include an Order entity, aiming to optimize order management, enhance customer experiences, and improve service quality. The updated representation includes interconnected entities like Product, Order, Customer, Employee, Transaction, and Inventory, aligning with industry standards and customer expectations



Data Flow Diagram (DFD)

The target DFD for Florace Bakery includes the Online Ordering system, enhancing its integration with existing processes like Order Fulfillment, Inventory Management, Product Baking, and Customer Interaction. This integration streamlines processes, improves customer engagement, and enhances operational excellence.
